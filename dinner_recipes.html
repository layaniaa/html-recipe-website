Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Bacon or pancetta
  • Eggs
  • Grated parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Salt & pepper
  • Parsley (optional)

Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. In a separate pan, cook bacon or pancetta until crispy. Whisk eggs, parmesan cheese, minced garlic, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Drain spaghetti and add it to the pan with bacon. Remove from heat and quickly stir in egg mixture. The heat from the pasta will cook the eggs. Serve immediately, garnished with chopped parsley if desired.

Grilled Steak

  • Steak (your choice of cut)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt & pepper

Preheat grill to high heat. Rub steak with olive oil and season generously with garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Grill steak for 4-5 minutes on each side for medium-rare, or longer depending on desired doneness. Let the steak rest for a few minutes before slicing. Serve hot.

Chicken Alfredo Pasta

  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Chicken breast, sliced
  • Heavy cream
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt & pepper
  • Parsley (optional)

Cook fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. In a separate pan, cook sliced chicken breast until no longer pink. In the same pan, melt butter and sauté minced garlic. Stir in heavy cream and grated parmesan cheese until sauce thickens. Season with salt and pepper. Toss cooked pasta with the sauce and chicken. Garnish with chopped parsley if desired and serve hot.

Baked Salmon

  • Salmon fillets
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic powder
  • Dill
  • Butter
  • Salt & pepper

Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Place salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Drizzle with lemon juice and melted butter. Sprinkle with garlic powder, dill, salt, and pepper. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork. Serve hot.
