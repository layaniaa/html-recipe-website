Tomato & Cucumber Salad

  • Tomatoes, cubed
  • Cucumber, sliced
  • Red onion, chopped
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt & pepper
  • Mint leaves (optional)

Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with mint leaves if desired. Toss gently to combine and serve.

Fruit Salad

  • Seasonal fruits like strawberries, mangoes, kiwi, grapes
  • Honey
  • Lemon juice
  • Fresh mint (optional)

Wash and chop fruits. Combine in a bowl. Drizzle with honey and lemon juice. Garnish with fresh mint leaves if desired. Toss gently to combine and serve.

Boiled Eggs with Toast

  • Eggs
  • Toasted bread
  • Butter
  • Salt & pepper

Boil eggs until cooked to desired doneness. Toast bread and spread with butter. Serve eggs with toasted bread. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Tuna Sandwich

  • Canned tuna
  • Lettuce
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Bread for sandwich
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt & pepper

Drain tuna and mix with mayonnaise, salt, and pepper. Spread tuna mixture on one slice of bread. Top with lettuce and sliced tomatoes. Cover with another slice of bread. Serve and enjoy!
