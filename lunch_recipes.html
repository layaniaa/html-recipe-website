Chicken Caesar Salad

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Grilled chicken breast
  • Croutons
  • Grated parmesan cheese
  • Caesar dressing

Combine lettuce, grilled chicken, croutons, and parmesan cheese in a large bowl. Drizzle with Caesar dressing. Toss gently to coat. Serve immediately.

Vegetable Stir Fry

  • Mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, snap peas, etc.)
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Cooking oil
  • Rice or noodles (optional)

Heat cooking oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Add garlic and ginger, stir until fragrant. Add mixed vegetables and stir fry until tender-crisp. Add soy sauce and sesame oil, toss to coat. Serve hot over rice or noodles if desired.

Grilled Salmon

  • Salmon fillets
  • Garlic powder
  • Paprika
  • Salt & pepper
  • Butter or olive oil
  • Lemon wedges

Season salmon fillets with garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Heat butter or olive oil in a grill pan over medium heat. Grill salmon for 4-5 minutes on each side or until cooked through. Serve with lemon wedges.

Veggie Pizza

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Assorted vegetables (bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives, etc.)
  • Grated mozzarella cheese
  • Italian seasoning
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). Roll out pizza dough and spread with pizza sauce. Top with vegetables and sprinkle with mozzarella cheese. Sprinkle with Italian seasoning and red pepper flakes if desired. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until crust is golden brown and cheese is melted and bubbly.
