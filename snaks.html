Caprese Salad Skewers

  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Fresh mozzarella balls
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Salt & pepper

Thread cherry tomatoes, mozzarella balls, and basil leaves onto skewers. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.

Guacamole and Chips

  • Ripe avocados
  • Lime juice
  • Red onion
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Tomato
  • Jalapeno
  • Salt & pepper
  • Tortilla chips

Mash avocados in a bowl and mix with lime juice. Stir in diced onion, chopped cilantro, diced tomato, and minced jalapeno. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve with tortilla chips.

Mini Quesadillas

  • Flour tortillas
  • Shredded cheese
  • Black beans
  • Corn
  • Chopped green onions
  • Salsa
  • Guacamole

Spread shredded cheese, black beans, corn, and chopped green onions on half of a tortilla. Fold the other half over to form a half-moon shape. Cook in a skillet over medium heat until cheese is melted and tortilla is golden brown. Serve with salsa and guacamole.

Vegetable Spring Rolls

  • Rice paper wrappers
  • Shredded carrots
  • Cucumber, julienned
  • Red bell pepper, julienned
  • Avocado, sliced
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Rice noodles, cooked
  • Peanut sauce

Soak rice paper wrappers in warm water until pliable. Lay flat and fill with shredded carrots, cucumber, red bell pepper, avocado, mint leaves, and cooked rice noodles. Roll tightly, tucking in the sides as you go. Serve with peanut sauce for dipping.
